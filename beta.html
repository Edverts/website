2. Setting up Android Play Store as a Developer

To join the Beta Program you'll need to set up your Android device Play Store as a developer - just follow these steps:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your Android device.
  2. Tap on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Go to Settings by tapping on it.
  4. Tap on the "About" section to expand it.
  5. Find the “Play Store Version” option at the bottom. Tap on it multiple times until you see a message that says you're now a developer.
  6. Go back to the main Settings page, and tap on the "General Settings" section to expand it.
  7. You should now see a new option called “Developer options”.
  8. Under "Developer options", find and tap on “Internal app sharing”.
  9. Toggle the switch to turn on Internal App Sharing. You might need to accept terms and conditions if prompted.
