Edverts

Welcome to Edverts, a mobile educational application.
This app is meant for your children.

Install it on their phone or tablet and let them play as much as they want, safe in the knowledge that they will not only play, but also improve their mental arithmetic!

Edverts will periodically ask them to solve a mathematical task, show them an explanation when they get it wrong or let them resume playing when they get it right.
